About Skyler

Skyler is currently an eight month old puppy. She might seem like she's still young but she weighs in at about 80 pounds right now! We expect her to be at least 110 by the time she is fully mature at the age of around a year and a half. Bernese mountain dogs overall have a tame personality and are very attached to their owners, with a tendency to be aloof towards strangers. However we are lucky enough that Skyler loves most people she meets, and loves to play with anyone who will give her attention.

Skyler's Training Goals

  • Sit nicely for petting - be able to accept attention from strangers without jumping.
  • Master sit, stay, down, come, paw, wait, take it, leave it, etc. in an area with distractions such as the park.
  • Recall commands with consistency.
  • Being able to approach strangers without Skyler trying to get attention from them before allowing the stranger and I to greet.
  • Good behavior during grooming including turning on command.
  • Walking through a crowd without pulling, acting polite in a highly trafficked area.
  • Stay with strangers politely and accept spending time away from us with little to no anxiety.
